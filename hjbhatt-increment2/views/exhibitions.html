Exhibitions

Reflections in Code

Explore a stunning collection of generative art pieces created entirely through algorithms and code. "Reflections in Code" features works by over 20 international artists who use programming languages as their creative medium. Each piece evolves in real time, offering a unique viewing experience with every visit. On display from March through August 2026.

The Analog Revival

In a world dominated by screens, "The Analog Revival" celebrates the beauty of handcrafted art forms — from letterpress printing to hand-drawn animation. This exhibition juxtaposes traditional techniques with their digital counterparts, inviting visitors to reflect on what is gained and lost in the shift to digital creation. Running from January through June 2026.

Echoes of Tomorrow

Step into an immersive audio-visual experience that imagines the cities, landscapes, and communities of the future. Using projection mapping, spatial audio, and AI-generated imagery, "Echoes of Tomorrow" transports visitors into speculative worlds shaped by climate change, urbanization, and technological advancement. A limited-run exhibition available from April through September 2026.
